- 4 STANCES -

Skate tricks in all 4 stances: Regular, Switch, Nollie, Fakie

The goal is to mirror Regular and Nollie to Switch and Fakie.
That's why Fakie is always the opposite direction. For example:
Regular FS 180, Nollie FS 180, Switch FS 180, Fakie BS 180
